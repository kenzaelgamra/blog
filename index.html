My experience with the E-nitiate Camp

I'm so proud to say that I took part in the first edition of the E-nitiate Camp summer camp. Its main objective was to introduce us (moroccan high school students) to key coding notions and concepts, but actually, it has also inspired me to further my research and discovery of programming beyond the camp. I've always been a very creative person, and this camp allows me to bring my craziest ideas to life which is just amazing. Almost as amazing as the volunteer's enthusiasm, their passion for coding, and, above all, the patience they had throughout the whole 10 days we've been in AUI. Dear E-nitiate Team, if you read this, I just want to thank you for being..You :) I also want to thank our great host, AlAkhawayn University, for making sure nothing was lacking.

E-nitiate was such a great experience, both at a human level (special shoutout to you 2016 e-nitiators !! I will miss you.. psychos ♡) and professionally (I've learned countless things, you can't even imagine).
